I've been thinking about which cruise I'd like to do next. As relaxing as it would be to go sailing across the mediterranean again, I've been enchanted by the pictures of the Norwegian fjords. The image of a cruise smoothly sailing across the vastness of the land, covered under a blanket of green forest, misted by the freshness of the air - it all seems very romantic.

I would most certainly splurge on a balcony cabin. And I would go in late spring or early summer, the kind of weather when you would surely enjoy a long walk, and sitting outside the cruise balcony wouldn't be too cold.
